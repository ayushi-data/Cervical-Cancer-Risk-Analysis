# Cervical Cancer Risk Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes risk factors associated with cervical cancer using a healthcare dataset. The goal is to identify relationships between behavioral, demographic, and medical factors and cervical cancer diagnosis.

## Tools Used
- Python (Pandas, NumPy, Seaborn, Matplotlib)
- Jupyter Notebook
- Power BI
- Data Cleaning & Exploratory Data Analysis

## Key Analysis
The dataset was cleaned and analyzed to explore relationships between:
- Age groups
- Smoking habits
- STD history
- HPV infection
- Cervical cancer diagnosis

## Dashboard Insights
Key insights from the analysis include:

- HPV infection shows the strongest association with cervical cancer diagnosis.
- Smoking and STD history correlate with increased diagnosis risk.
- Higher case concentration appears in younger and middle age groups.
